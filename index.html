About

Our approach is not to defy the system but to befriend it by building the bridge of films for stories to find the studios to reach the larger and wider audience for the benefit of all parties involved to share and cherish its success together.

While others focus only on producing films as a business We, besides doing the same, aim to produce filmmakers (Writers, Directors, DOPs, Producers, Editors, Production Designers, Music Directors, & so on.) and create a novelty (of talents) that can be a force to reckon with.
