It’s not a typical film production house formed by the founders and led by the bosses. It’s a platform of the people by the people and for the people, where everyone, from film enthusiasts to film aspirants, from cine-goers to outsiders can come forward with a story that is worth telling and join the tribe to make good films together.

About

It’s a cinematic congregation for the voiceless to find their voice, for the rejected to find their opportunity, for the hopeless to find their strength, to join, participate and practice the joy pain and adventure of filmmaking without any bias, boundaries or fear for the greater good of cinema.

Our story is your story and together we can make it a universal story, for a film without a story is unimaginable, and for that we must take an oath together to maintain the cinematic integrity in quest for good stories to make good films, both critically and commercially, domestically as well as internationally, and consistently.

Vision

Our approach is not to defy the system but to befriend it by building the bridge of films for stories to find the studios to reach the larger and wider audience for the benefit of all parties involved to share and cherish its success together.

With the lines getting blurred between the small and big screens these days, the scope of excellence in creative services holds no boundaries and has no limits.

Services
